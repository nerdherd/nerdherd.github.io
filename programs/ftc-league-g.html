FTC League G

Nerd Herd 687 runs FIRST Technical Challenge (FTC) teams and competitions for students at the California Academy of Mathematics and Science (CAMS). This program invites students from 9th-12th grade to participate as part of one of our two FTC teams, Team 49 and 51.

IMAGE HERE

FTC League G was created by CAMS in 2022 to hold FTC competitions on their campus. Its main purpose was to allow for the ease of transportation for teams in the Long Beach Area and move the competition day from Sunday to Saturday where most people are available.

IMAGE HERE

Team 48:

FTC Team #48 NerdHerd Locked in, started their rookie year in FTC with strong accomplishments, competing in six competitions. This team placed 1st place at Socal FTC League G – Meet 0, 3rd place at Socal FTC League G – Meet 1, and 3rd place at Socal FTC League G – Meet 2. This team used a linear actuator to pick up the specimen and place them on the low rungs. This team also had two autonomous code that they ran at competitions pushing the samples into the zones. During Teleop, they also achieved level one ascent. During ILT, this team performed extraordinary defense against some great teams. However, we weren’t able to move on but we did get a second chance as we were invited to the Wild Card Tournament to prove ourselves one more time. At the Wild Card Tournament, FTC 48 Locked received the 2nd place Think Award for amazing FTC portfolio and documentation. After the 2025 Into the Deep Season, CAMS has decided to discontinue Team 48 from FTC and give more help to Teams 49 and 51 for more hands on deck, instead of dividing and conquering!

Team 49:

Our FTC Team #49 Seven Squared, had profound achievements from the year 2023-2025 not only dominating on the field during their competition, but also their contribution to bringing back to the community through their various outreach events hosted. Starting off as a newbie team in the 2023 Centerstage, FTC #49 won second place competing at Meet 2 in the California, SoCal FTC League G, in addition to winning Finalist Alliance award, 3rd place for Control Award, winning Design Award, and our member Owen Sun winning Dean’s List Semifinalist in ILT7. Advancing to the California Socal FTC Championship they won the Promote Award ending of their first season.


Moving onto 2024 Into the Deep, Team 49 dominated the field winning 2nd place in Socal FTC League G – Meet 1, 1st place in Socal FTC League G – Meet 2, and 2st place in Socal FTC League G – Meet 3. Later advancing to ILT6, our team won 2nd place while also winning Inspire Award, Alliance Captain, and our member Jiwon Jung winning Dean’s List Semifinalist. Ending off the season with Canadian Rockies Premier Events in July 2025, our team won 2nd place for Think Award, and 1st pick as Finalist Alliance.


Follow Team 49 on Instagram at @cams_ftc49
